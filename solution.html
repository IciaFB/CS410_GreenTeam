The Solution

A real-time AI-powered translator will be developed to enable seamless cross-language communication. Deliver a mobile application to use user data to power AI prompts that deliver high quality and context aware translations.


Type of Software

AI-powered mobile application that connects with a Bluetooth earpiece for hands-free, real-time translation.


High-Level Functionality

  • Instantly translate spoken language with minimal latency.
  • Support both widely spoken languages and regional dialects.
  • Unlike smartphone apps, it will provide hands-free conversation without the need to pause, type, or look at a screen.
  • The system will incorporate context-aware AI to enhance translation accuracy by understanding idioms, slang, and cultural nuances.
  • Able to use a Bluetooth headset or earpiece to have discrete translations.
